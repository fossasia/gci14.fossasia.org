About

FOSSASIA is the Free and Open Source technology organization developing software and hardware. The annual FOSSASIA Summit draws particpants and attendees from around the world. Projects at FOSSASIA range from open hardware, to design, graphics and software. FOSSASIA was established in 2009 by Hong Phuc DANG and Mario Behling
